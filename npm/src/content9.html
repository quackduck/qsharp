The exact behavior of this operation called RunExample depends on the quantum simulator or processor you are using.

On the simulator used in these demos, this function prints the information on each basis state that has a non-zero amplitude, one basis state per row. This includes information about the amplitude of the state, the probability of measuring that state, and the phase of the state.
